About Us

Cafe del Sol is a cafe for people who want good coffee, proper lighting and a perfect ambiance. We serve homemade soups and sandwiches that are made in our kitchen. Cafe del Sol is as its name suggests, right next to the beach while being in the middle of the Old Town. Enjoy a great view of the sea from which you can have your coffee or ice-cream treat.
